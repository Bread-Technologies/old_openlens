## What OpenLens Does

OpenLens tracks your brand's presence across five major AI platforms:

- **Visibility by platform** — How often you get mentioned, measured as a percentage of relevant queries
- **Attribute breakdown** — What each AI model thinks about your strengths and weaknesses
- **Source intelligence** — Every URL that AI models cite when discussing your industry
- **Competitive comparison** — Share of voice, sentiment, and positioning vs. competitors
- **Full response archive** — The exact words each AI said, searchable and filterable
